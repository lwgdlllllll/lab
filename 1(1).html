Student Research Directions

Research Areas

Students can participate in the following sustainable technology-related research projects:

Renewable Energy Efficiency

Research new methods to improve solar and wind energy conversion efficiency, such as perovskite solar cells, high-altitude wind power technology, etc.

Battery Technology

Explore safer, higher-capacity battery materials, such as solid-state batteries, sodium-ion batteries, metal-air batteries, etc.

Smart City Algorithms

Develop intelligent algorithms for optimizing urban resource allocation, such as traffic flow prediction, energy demand management, etc.

Sustainable Materials

Research new biodegradable or recyclable materials, such as bio-based plastics, self-healing materials, etc.

Research Resources

  • Ministry of Science and Technology Sustainable Development Technology Special Projects
  • National Natural Science Foundation related projects
  • University Sustainable Development Research Centers
  • Corporate Green Technology Laboratories
  • International organization cooperation projects (such as UNDP)

Recommended Research Topics

  • AI-based wind farm layout optimization research
  • Electric vehicle retired battery cascade utilization technology research
  • Urban building energy consumption intelligent monitoring and optimization system design
  • Blockchain-based carbon trading platform construction
  • New high-efficiency low-cost photovoltaic material development
